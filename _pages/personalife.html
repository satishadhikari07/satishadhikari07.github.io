Some Book Recommendations

The listed books have deeply influenced me in terms of my work, discipline, and helped me to build an amazing perspective on the world and humanity.

"Atomic Habits" highlights the power of small, consistent good habits that has impact over period of time. It explains how tiny habits, small change in good decisions if built effectively, can lead to remarkable transformation.

"Homo Sapiens" explores the history of humankind from the emergence of our species to the modern era. It highlights how cognitive, agricultural, and scientific revolutions shaped societies, beliefs, and power structures.
